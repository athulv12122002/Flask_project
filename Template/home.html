Cassava Leaf Disease Identification

Cassava is the second-largest food crop in Sub-Saharan Africa after maize. Over 500 million people in Africa consume cassava daily, making it one of the continent's most important staple foods. Cassava consists of leaf images for the cassava plant depicting healthy and four (4) disease conditions:

  • Cassava Mosaic Disease (CMD)
  • Cassava Bacterial Blight (CBB)
  • Cassava Green Mite (CGM)
  • Cassava Brown Streak Disease (CBSD)

The dataset consists of a total of 21,395 labeled images, which are split into a training set, a test set, and a validation set. The number of images per class are unbalanced, with the two disease classes CMD and CBSD having 72% of the images.
